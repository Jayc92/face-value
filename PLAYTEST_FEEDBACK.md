## Understanding
1. After your first round, what did you think the **goal** of the game was?
2. What, if anything, **confused** you?
3. Did you understand that **trivia earns the credits** you bid with?
4. Did you understand what a **reserve** is on the Bidding Floor?
5. On the Results screen, did you understand **why you won or lost**?

## Feel
6. Did the **auction feel fair**? Too easy, too hard, or about right?
7. Did the **onboarding** help, get in the way, or not matter?
8. Did you **want to play again** right after your first round? Why / why not?

## Screens & rewards
9. Which screen looked **weakest** or most confusing?
10. Which **reward** felt best (winning a seat, a medal, a PB, the streak)?
11. What would make you **come back tomorrow**?
